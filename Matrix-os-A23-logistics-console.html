How this works

This console talks to your matrix-OS-A22-logistics.py service:
  • GET /api/logistics/start – starts periodic tasks
  • GET /api/logistics/status – shows current status
  • GET /api/logistics/stop – placeholder response (daemon threads)
Keep the A22 server running to maintain background maintenance (cleanup, AI upkeep, data sync).
